Donations

Coffee Alliance for the Arts programs focus especially on all young artists in our community, including Coffee High School Arts Week; a scholarship for a senior at Coffee High School or CCA; a Valdosta State Children’s Theatre performance for all first graders; Munch & Music at Emma Ward Park in April; and Missoula Children’s Theatre every June; and Lanterns & Legacies in August.

If you want your donation to support a particular program like Missoula Children’s Theatre, please indicate that directive on your check.

Designated Activities to Support

  • Arts Week

  • Classic Movie Night

  • Coffee Alliance for the Arts Scholarship

  • Fine Arts & Communicative Arts Student of the Month

  • Lanterns, Legacies, & Lies

  • Missoula Children's Theatre

  • Munch & Music
