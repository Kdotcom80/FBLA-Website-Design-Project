Come and join us!

Coffee Alliance for the Arts is a non-profit organization dedicated to offering art enrichment experiences to the residents of Coffee County and its neighboring areas.

You can become a member of Coffee Alliance for the Arts for only $30. The meetings are once a month at 6:00 PM on Thursdays at the Chamber of Commerce.

What about scholarships?

Coffee Alliance for the Arts offers a $500 college scholarship to current high school senior students who are planning to major in art-related careers.

Congratulations to last year's scholarship winner, Keller Phillips. He is attending South Georgia State College!

Events too?

Yup! Events too!
We hold numerous events and programs that students of all ages can attend and participate in.
Students get to have the opportunity to play a role in multiple performances of their liking.
