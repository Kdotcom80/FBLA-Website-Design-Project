About Us

Coffee Alliance for the Arts is a non-profit organization, who are deeply committed to enhancing the cultural landscape of Coffee County and its neighboring areas. Our primary focus revolves around providing enrichment experiences for people of all ages within our community. Through a variety of engaging and accessible programs, we aim to foster creativity, inspire a love for the arts, and contribute to the overall well-being and cultural vibrancy of our local population.

Our mission

Our mission for the Coffee Alliance for the Arts is to cultivate, promote,
and elevate the understanding and engagement with the arts in Coffee County, Georgia.
